Welcome!

Hello! Welcome to my personal website. Here you can learn more about me, my profession and technical experience, and my involvement in my communities. This website was written by me using HTML, JavaScript, and CSS, and there is even a toggle button in the top right corner that allows you to see this website in a different CSS style. This website is one of the projects I have to do in a class I am taking right now called CSCE 315 - Programming Studio.

I am a junior (c/o '24) Computer Science major and Statistics minor at Texas A&M University, but I was born and raised in Sugar Land, Texas, which is located in the greater Houston area. I attended Clements High School and graduated in 2020 during the pandemic which was actually when I heavily considered my options as a future undergrad student and ended up deciding to pursue Mechanical Engineering. During my freshman year in college, however, as I was applying through the Entry-To-A-Major process, I decided that programming is where my passions lie and changed my academic path to Computer Science instead. This was one of the best decisions I have ever made in my life. Now, here I am starting my third year in the major and have already accomplished many things I never could have dreamed of. I recently completed my Summer internship with Hewlett-Packard Enterprise as a Software Developer Intern, which you can read more about in the portfolio tab, and I am both the President of Aggie Women in Computer Science (AWICS) and a member of the Society of Women Engineers (SWE) organizations here at Texas A&M. I also like to help my community in both the College Station and Sugar Land areas, which you can learn more about in the Service tab.

I hope this website gives you ample information about me, but if it doesn't, you can always check out my social media handles (at the bottom of every page) or just email me. I hope you enjoy reading through my website as much as I had making it!
